A heat mat is a flat warming pad placed under or beside a tank to keep the water at a steady, comfortable temperature. Sea Monkeys (brine shrimp) grow best in warm, stable water, and a heat mat can help maintain that temperature—especially in colder rooms or during winter. Benefits of using a heat mat: 1. Steady Temperature: Sea Monkeys do best around 72–80°F (22–27°C). A heat mat helps prevent sudden temperature drops that can stress or slow them down. 2. Faster Hatching: Warmer, stable temperatures can help Sea Monkey eggs hatch more quickly and evenly. 3. Better Activity Levels: Sea Monkeys tend to be more active, swim more, and grow faster when kept in a comfortable warm range. 4. Healthier Algae Growth: If you’re growing algae as a food source, warm water can help it grow at a steady rate (as long as it’s not too hot). 5. Gentle, Even Heat: Heat mats warm the tank gradually and evenly, unlike some lamps that can overheat the water from the top.

TIP: Because Sea Monkey tanks are small, only low-level warmth is needed. The goal is gentle, consistent heat—not hot water. Using the heat mat with a low setting or a thermostat helps keep everything safe and stable..
